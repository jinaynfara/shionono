Lesson Proper

Vocabulary

The vocabulary module consists of nouns that are branched into core pronouns, emotions, time, places, people and body parts which can be considered the most necessary words when interacting with Koreans. These words are very helpful as it will aid you in understanding almost 50% of the Korean words.



Let's Learn: Nouns

Nouns are a great way to start understanding the Korean language. Once you learn these words, you can start to understand about 50% of all Korean words!

Let's Learn: Verbs

Casual verb endings: 가다 → 가 (ga)
Polite verb endings: 가다 → 가요 (gayo)
Formal verb endings: 가다 → 갑니다 (gamnida)
