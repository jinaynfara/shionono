Courses We Offer

Shionono provides the modules where you can start to learn basic Korean grammar, vocabulary, and writing.

Phonetics

The phonetics module consists of basic linguistics that deals with the pronunciation of Hangul which involves the movement of lips, tongue and teeth in producing the sounds for a particular character or letter. Let us begin our journey in learning the Koreanâ€™s way of speaking.

Writing

The writing strokes module will be focused on the basic handwriting of the consonants and vowels in the Korean Hangul. This includes the correct order of strokes whether being vertical, horizontal, or a curve. Get your pens and papers ready as we get into the next step in learning the Korean Hangul.

Vocabulary

The vocabulary module consists of nouns that are branched into core pronouns, time, places, and more which can be considered the most necessary words when interacting with Koreans. These words are very helpful as it will aid you in understanding almost 50% of the Korean words.

Grammar

The grammar module will be focused on the arrangement of words such as subject, object, and verb, especially the additional words in different kinds of sentences. This will provide you the next step in being able to understand the Hangul from speech and will help develop your skills in conversing using Hangul.
