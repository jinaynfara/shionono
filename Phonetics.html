Let's Learn: Phonetics

Phonetics is a branch of linguistics that deals with the pronunciation of a language which involves the movement of lips, tongue and teeth in producing the sounds for a particular character or letter. This is the first step in learning the Korean language as there are differences in the Filipino and Koreans’ way of speaking.
